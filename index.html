FAQs

Your questions answered

What exactly is a free audit and what does it involve?

+

A free audit is a 60-minute deep dive into your digital presence — we look at your delivery platform listings, Google Business profile, social media pages, and if you share it, your POS data. You walk away with a written findings report and a prioritised action list. No obligation to work with us after.

Do I need to sign a long-term contract?

+

No. All our plans are month-to-month. We ask for 30 days notice to cancel. We believe if we're doing our job well, you won't want to leave.

How much time will this take from me every month?

+

One 45-minute call per month. That's it. We handle everything else — you just need to be on that call to share updates, new menu items, or upcoming events.

Do you work with businesses outside Goa?

+

We have clients in London, Dubai, Mumbai, and Dublin who own businesses remotely. If you're outside Goa, we can still help but our strongest expertise is in the Goa market. Reach out and we'll be honest about whether we're the right fit.

What POS systems do you work with?

+

We've worked with Petpooja, Posist, Lightspeed, Square, and several custom systems. As long as you can export your data, we can work with it. If you don't have a POS system yet, we can recommend one too.

How quickly do you get started after signing up?

+

Within 5 working days. We run an onboarding session, get access to your platforms, and begin your first full month of management. You'll receive your first monthly report within 30 days of starting.

Is the ad spend included in the plan price?

+

No — ad spend is paid directly by you to the platform. Our fee covers the management, strategy, and execution. This keeps things transparent — you always know exactly where your money is going.

What kind of results can I realistically expect?

+

Our clients typically see measurable improvement in delivery platform orders within the first 60 days, and meaningful revenue growth within 90. One client saw +89% revenue growth year-on-year. Results vary based on your starting point.

Still have a question? We'll answer it.
